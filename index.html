Hello. Welcome to my site! I am Ellie Near.

I am a trans girl. I am a musician, writer, artist, web developer, game developer, engineer and hobbyist of a lot of other things. I love modding games, and I am currently creating a Quake mod, making soundtracks for multiple video games and writing a TTRPG with a friend.

You can see my works and take a look around on this website, although it might be kind of barren as of right now as I am in the process of trying to find some of my works that I have not archived proper.

The end goal of this site is to serve as some sort of repository of any productive work I have done, whether it be music, art, writing, etc. If you have any questions or just want to talk to me, contact me on Discord!
